Our Mission

Transparency in every donation

Daan was built to solve a simple but persistent problem: donors don't know where their money goes. We fix that.

The Problem

Why Daan exists

India has over 3 million NGOs — yet most donors have no way to verify whether their contribution actually reached the intended cause. Opaque processes, lack of digital infrastructure, and mistrust have held back philanthropic giving for decades.

Daan (दान — the Sanskrit word for charitable giving) was created by students at Manipal Institute of Technology to bridge this gap with technology: a platform where every donation is tracked, every rupee is reported, and every NGO is verified.

The Platform

Three actors, one ecosystem

🤝

Donors

Register, browse verified NGOs, donate money or materials, and read impact reports on how their contributions were used.

🏛️

NGOs

Register with verifiable credentials, list their specific needs, acknowledge donations, and publish utilization reports.

🛡️

Admins

Verify NGO registrations, manage complaints, monitor platform integrity, and ensure all actors follow transparent processes.
